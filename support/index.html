Dashcam App Support Guide (v1)

Last updated: 2026-03-29 Contact: legal@magsafedashcam.com

Quick Support Summary

  • Current prelaunch physical validation device: iPhone 16 Pro (iPhone17,1) on iOS 26.4.
  • Launch-day public guidance is recent non-mini iPhones on iOS 16+.
  • Recording is foreground-only. If you leave the app or hand off to another maps app, recording stops.
  • iCloud upload is currently beta/experimental and depends on your Apple iCloud configuration and provisioning.
  • Clips stay stored locally in Dashcam Library unless you export or enable optional upload behavior.

Recommended Device Setup

  1. Mount the phone securely in landscape or portrait where the rear camera has a clear road view.
  2. Keep the screen unlocked while recording.
  3. Use rear-camera mode for the best sustained thermal and battery behavior.
  4. If you rely on uploads, keep the phone on power and Wi-Fi when possible.

Known Product Constraints

  • Background recording is not supported on iPhone. Recording stops when the app backgrounds.
  • Launching Apple Maps, Google Maps, or Waze from inside the app stops recording after a warning.
  • Dual camera and live preview features can add heat and are not the recommended baseline for long drives.
  • Upload beta may remain unavailable on Personal Team builds until paid-team provisioning is configured.

Troubleshooting

Recording will not start

  • Free device storage and try again.
  • Confirm camera permission is allowed.
  • If the phone is face-down, the app may show a non-blocking advisory before recording continues.

Map is not centered on my location

  • Open Drive Mode and allow location access when prompted.
  • Tap the recenter control after the first GPS fix if needed.
  • If location is denied or restricted, Drive Mode cannot follow your position.

Uploads are queued but not leaving the device

  • Check whether Upload Beta is enabled in Settings.
  • If Wi-Fi only is enabled, connect to Wi-Fi.
  • If Require charging is enabled, connect power.
  • If the status shows container unavailable, leave the clips queued and retry later after iCloud/provisioning is available.

Library is locked

  • Use device authentication to unlock the local library when Library Lock is enabled.
