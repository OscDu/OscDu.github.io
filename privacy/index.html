Dashcam App Privacy Policy (v1)

Last updated: 2026-02-15 Effective date: 2026-02-15

This Privacy Policy describes how MagSafe Dashcam LLC ("Company," "we," "us") handles information in connection with the Dashcam mobile application (the "App").

Contact: legal@magsafedashcam.com

1. Scope

This policy covers information processed through the App. It does not cover third-party services you access separately.

The v1 release is intended for users outside the European Union, United Kingdom, European Economic Area, and Switzerland.

2. Information We Process

Depending on your settings and use, the App may process:

  • video recordings created by you;
  • optional audio recordings (if enabled);
  • optional location and speed metadata (if enabled);
  • recording timestamps and clip metadata;
  • security/integrity hashes for clip verification;
  • app configuration settings.

3. Where Processing Occurs

Core processing occurs on your device.

If you enable iCloud upload, selected clips are copied to your Apple iCloud container under your Apple account. We do not operate a separate Company-hosted clip backend in v1.

4. How We Use Information

We process information to:

  • record and manage clips;
  • apply retention and protection settings;
  • prepare exports and integrity checks;
  • run optional upload flows you enable;
  • provide app functionality, security, and troubleshooting.

5. Legal Bases and Consent Model

Where consent is required by law, you provide it through platform permissions and in-app settings (for example, camera, microphone, location, notifications).

You control optional metadata/audio features in the App settings.

6. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information.

We do not use third-party ad networks in v1.

You may share recordings yourself through export/share features. You are responsible for lawful sharing and for obtaining any required consent from affected individuals.

7. Retention

Retention is primarily controlled by your configured loop duration and protection settings.

Protected clips may be retained until you remove protection or delete them.

Export artifacts may temporarily exist in app temporary storage during export processing.

iCloud-retained copies are governed by your iCloud account/storage and your actions in Apple services.

8. Security

The App includes security controls such as:

  • local library access gating (when enabled);
  • optional re-authentication before protected exports;
  • integrity hashing for clips and sidecar metadata;
  • privacy-shield behavior when the app is not active.

No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure.

9. Your Choices

You can:

  • toggle audio/location/speed collection;
  • toggle iCloud upload and Wi-Fi-only behavior;
  • toggle metadata export stripping and warnings;
  • delete clips from your library;
  • disable optional features in settings.

10. Children

The App is intended for adults (18+) and is not directed to children.

11. Region-Specific Notes

California residents may have rights under applicable California privacy laws, subject to legal thresholds, exemptions, and applicability.

If you submit a verifiable privacy request, contact legal@magsafedashcam.com with sufficient detail for us to evaluate and respond under applicable law.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Material changes are effective as of the updated "Last updated" date, and additional notice may be provided where required by law.
